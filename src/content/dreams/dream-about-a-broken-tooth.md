title: "Dream About a Broken Tooth"
---

Dreaming about a broken tooth symbolizes insecurity, stress, or fear of losing control. Teeth represent confidence, communication, and personal power. When a tooth breaks in a dream, your subconscious is highlighting a situation where you feel vulnerable or unprepared.

If the tooth breaks suddenly, the dream may reflect **unexpected stress** — a sudden change, conflict, or emotional shock.

If the tooth cracks slowly, it may symbolize **long-term pressure** or a situation that has been wearing you down over time.

If you feel embarrassed in the dream, it may reflect **concern about how others perceive you**. You may be worried about appearance, performance, or judgment.

If you feel pain, the dream may symbolize **emotional hurt** or unresolved tension.

Overall, a broken tooth dream encourages you to examine your confidence, stress levels, and the areas of life where you feel fragile or exposed.
