title: "Dream About a Broken Bridge"
---

Dreaming about a broken bridge symbolizes disconnection, obstacles, or emotional distance between you and a goal, person, or part of yourself. Bridges represent connection and transition. When the bridge is broken, your subconscious is highlighting a gap that feels difficult to cross.

If you stand at the edge of the broken bridge, the dream may reflect **hesitation or uncertainty**. You may feel unsure how to move forward.

If you try to cross the broken bridge, it may symbolize **determination despite challenges**. You are willing to take risks to reach what matters.

If the bridge collapses as you cross, the dream may reflect **fear of failure** or anxiety about a major life transition.

If you find another way around, the dream symbolizes **adaptability and resilience**. You can still reach your destination, even if the original path is blocked.

Overall, a broken bridge dream highlights the need to reassess your path and find new ways to connect or move forward.
