title: "Dream About a Broken Mirror"
---

Dreaming about a broken mirror symbolizes identity concerns, self-image issues, or emotional fragmentation. Mirrors represent reflection — how you see yourself, how you believe others see you, and the truth you hold about your inner world. When the mirror breaks, your subconscious is highlighting a disruption in your sense of self.

If you see your reflection shatter, the dream may reflect **self-doubt, insecurity, or a fear of change**. You may be questioning who you are or how you fit into a situation.

If someone else breaks the mirror, it may symbolize **external criticism or conflict**. You may feel judged, misunderstood, or pressured to change.

If the mirror breaks accidentally, it may represent **unexpected change** — a shift in identity, relationships, or personal beliefs.

If you feel calm while looking at the broken mirror, the dream may symbolize **transformation**. You may be shedding an old identity and stepping into a new version of yourself.

Overall, a broken mirror dream invites you to examine your self-perception and embrace the parts of yourself that are evolving.
