# Shadow

The rejected, hidden, or undeveloped parts of the self. The Shadow in a dream appears as a threatening figure, a dark double, or a pursuer. Engaging rather than fleeing it leads to integration.

## Key Themes

- Examine the emotional tone of the dream when this symbol appeared
- Consider how this symbol relates to current life circumstances
- Reflect on your personal associations with shadow

## Questions for Reflection

1. How did you feel when you encountered this symbol in the dream?
2. What in your waking life mirrors the energy of this symbol?
3. Is this symbol asking you to embrace, release, or transform something?
